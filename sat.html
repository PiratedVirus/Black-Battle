Students’ Alumni Team, famously called SAT is the team that coordinates various activities of Alumni Association of Government Engineering College Aurangabad (AAGECA) with the college and past students.

Objectives

  • To provide a platform for connecting the alumni and students of the college.
  • Channelizing the effort of the alumni towards the efforts of the alumni towards the betterment of students.
  • To work for collecting and organising the required professional information of alumni for purpose.

    • Values

  • Service to Govt. College of Engineering Aurangabad
  • A mutually beneficial relationship with the Alumni Association of Govt. Engineering College Aurangabad (AAGECA)
  • Connections and interactions with the GECA alumni across various cross sections of society .
  • SAT gives Leadership opportunities to students along with teamwork experience to its members.
  • Diversity via co-curricular activities

    • SAT is working with “ building on ” the joy of togetherness. It strives to build students future. Since its foundation in 2008, SAT is volunteering students’ time to connect college community through students-alumni engagement events.

    SAT comprises of 30-35 members representing all branches of college led by a head-coordinators and vice-coordinators.

    SAT offers its members unparalleled leadership opportunities through events and networking with alumni. The bonds created between members as well as the Alumni Association has proved invaluable as many SAT members have created long lasting friendship and networking bonds.

    AAGECA and SAT have conducted various successful events such as Google Startup Weekend, Toppers Felicitation, and Educational Assistance.

    SAT has played a vital role in organising Global Alumni Meet 2015 as well, Online communication, telephonic contact has helped to connect almost 4000 Alumni with college.

    SAT members, since foundation have built their management and teamwork skills through many such events . SAT is a team in a true sense meaning Together Everyone Achieves More. SAT has now became an inseparable part of GECA and AAGECA.
