1987

Computer Science

The Department places high priority on establishing and maintaining innovative research programs that enhance the educational opportunity and encourage a broad base of extramural support to prepare future generations of computer professionals for long term careers in research, technical development and applications


The Computer Science department boasts of very highly equipped labs in the campus, the systems run on newly released version of Windows as well as open source OS like Ubuntu.

There is a small caveat and that is the systems are and of low configuration and needs maintenance frequently (very frequently) and this is the only problem of the Computer Science department.

The electronic kits provided in the Electronics lab are not

Coffee Shop
& Garden

Coffee shop along with a garden located in the middle of the college provides refreshment to the students during their college hours. Hygienic foods, coffee, tea, snacks are available at affordable prices. The garden is a source of relaxation for the students.


Gymkhana

Place situated near hostel creates an environment that gives warmth and comfort. It develops health and maintain fitness of students of the institute.

Courts

The playgrounds

Badminton court

Situated near CC is a platform for students to play games like badminton,table tennis,carrom,chess.students can play in the morning and evening hours to enhance their sports skills and have a break from their academics.It is an efficiently managed place and is in proximity to the hostels.

Basketball and volleyball court

Both are located in front of workshop in proximity to each other and are used by the students during the morning and evening hours to play basketball and volleyball.Regular proper maintainence of the grounds is done.It provides a great platform for students to develop their sport skills.

Auditorium

Recently renovated infrastructure situated above central library.It has a capacity of 900 seats.Cultural events and guests lectures are held here

CC Katta

A beautifully maintained place with greenery all around with provision to sit and relax

Central Library

Well equipped asset of the college.it provides facilities like book bank,e-journals,newspapers,magazines,thesis etc which help students for their academics and other competitive exams
