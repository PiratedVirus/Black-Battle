    • Let's connect the Alma dots...

    Alumni association of Government College of Engineering, Aurangabad (AAGECA) fosters the link between students and the alumni. It is a step taken towards creating various opportunities for the students. They can get help from the alumnus for various activities like internship programs, project guidance for Students, Sponsorship, Student-aid scheme, Prize distribution events etc.

    Read More
    Aim #1

    AAGECA is an organization through which the alumni of the institute can keep in touch with the students, faculty, and staff of the institute, the activities of the institute and with one another.

    Aim #2

    AAGECA enables the institute to help in the different assistance required by the industry. It also provides testing and consultancy to the Alumni of the college in different fields of Engineering and Technology.

    Aim #3

    Arrange any activity for the benefit of students, alumni and the faculty of the institute. AAGEGA helps students of the institute on basis of merit in the form of scholarship.
